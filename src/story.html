Our Story!

We started our journey two years ago in Saint George, Utah. At first we only handled family events, such as weddings and parties. This was for the most part a fun thing to do, but we saw a spark!

The large amount of compliments at these events gave us the confidence to finally proceed with a much larger vision. We now do Charcuterie boards all over Utah, from Logan to Saint George we are serving the best Charcuterie that money can buy.

We source high quality ingredients from every available store that has things such as imported meats and cheeses, exotic fruits, etc. The fun part is that what ends up on the board is all up to you! We work closely with clients to accomplish a delicious and unforgettable evening.
